Hello! I'm an incoming first year PhD student at CMU advised by Keenan Crane in the Geometry Collective. My reasearch interests are in Computer Graphics, specifically in Geometry Processing. I'm grateful to be supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program.

Previously, I did my undergrad in Computer Science and Mathematics at MIT, where I worked with Professor Justin Solomon in the Geometric Data Processing Group. During past summers, I've interned at Walt Disney Animation Studios with Rasmus Tamstorf and at University of Toronto with Alec Jacobson in the Dynamic Graphics Project.

In my spare time I like cooking, coding interesting things, climbing mountains, and crocheting.
